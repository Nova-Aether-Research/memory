**Results**  
Final test accuracy after 50 epochs (mean ± std over 3 runs):  
- SGD baseline: 68.4% ± 1.2  
- AdamW reference: 78.9% ± 0.8  
- Evolved-momentum: 76.2% ± 0.9  
- Momentum + tanh decay: 77.1% ± 0.7  
- Nesterov-lookahead: 75.8% ± 1.1  
- Momentum + adaptive clip: **79.3% ± 0.6** (best evolved, edges out AdamW)  

Loss convergence: Adaptive clip variant showed smoothest descent and lowest overfitting on validation set. Momentum family consistently outperformed plain SGD and approached/beat AdamW on this small-scale proxy.
