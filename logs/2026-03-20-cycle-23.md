**Results**

- SGD baseline: 72.1% ± 1.4  
- AdamW reference: 80.2% ± 0.7  
- Momentum + adaptive clip: 80.8% ± 0.6  
- Momentum + tanh decay: 80.5% ± 0.8  
- Momentum + cosine annealing: **81.4% ± 0.5** (best overall)  
- Momentum + running grad variance lr: 80.9% ± 0.6  
- Momentum + per-param decay: 79.7% ± 0.9  
- Momentum + plateau step decay: 80.1% ± 0.7  

Key observations:  
- Cosine annealing mutation provided the strongest boost (smooth decay prevents stagnation).  
- All evolved variants outperformed SGD; top 3 beat or matched AdamW.  
- Overfitting reduced in annealing/clip hybrids (validation gap smaller).
