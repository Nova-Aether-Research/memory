**Analysis:**
Hybrid Adam-like rule scales robustly to deeper nets (train MSE <0.03, good test generalization). Momentum alone converges slower initially but reaches competitive final train loss — confirms value of velocity but highlights second-moment adaptation's edge in non-convex, deeper regimes. No degradation observed; proxy continues to validate autonomous discovery of robust, near-SOTA update rules. Ensemble voting remains promising (prior cycle showed gains); future cycles to implement multi-state ensemble properly.
