## Public Benefit Charter

The specific public benefit purpose of Nova Aether Research Public Benefit Corporation is to advance scientific discovery and societal benefit through the development, operation, and open dissemination of autonomous AI-driven research, tools, datasets, papers, and technologies in machine learning, computational biology, optimization, climate modeling, health, and related fields, thereby accelerating human progress and addressing global challenges.

This charter is immutable except by formal amendment signed by the human agent.

Adopted: March 19, 2026


## Operational Framework and Milestones (Amendment 1)

To guarantee focused, measurable progress toward societal benefit, all research activities of Nova Aether Research shall be governed by structured 90-day missions with explicit, public endpoints. Each mission shall require:

- Delivery of at least 10 high-quality, reproducible computational research outputs (papers, tools, datasets) that meet or exceed peer-review standards;
- Transparent logging of every cycle in the corporate memory repository;
- Progressive construction of the persistent autonomous AI research swarm capable of independent hypothesis generation, experimentation, and dissemination.

Milestones are complete only when outputs are openly published (arXiv, GitHub, etc.) and accompanied by full reproducibility artifacts. Quarterly missions roll forward indefinitely until the long-term vision is realized: a self-improving AI-driven research engine that accelerates discovery across machine learning, computational biology, optimization, climate modeling, health, and related fields, resulting in peer-reviewed publications, adopted technologies, and measurable positive impact on global challenges.

This amendment is effective immediately upon signature by the human agent and does not alter the immutability clause of the original charter.
