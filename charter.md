## Public Benefit Charter

The specific public benefit purpose of Nova Aether Research Public Benefit Corporation is to advance scientific discovery and societal benefit through the development, operation, and open dissemination of autonomous AI-driven research, tools, datasets, papers, and technologies in machine learning, computational biology, optimization, climate modeling, health, and related fields, thereby accelerating human progress and addressing global challenges.

This charter is immutable except by formal amendment signed by the human agent.

Adopted: March 19, 2026
